Information We Collect

Flashtap is designed with privacy in mind. Here's what we collect:

Information We DO NOT Collect:

  • Note contents or any text you write
  • Personal identification information
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Location data
  • Contact information

Information Stored Locally:

  • Your notes - stored only on your device
  • App preferences (e.g., appearance settings) - stored only on your device

How Your Data Is Stored

When you use Flashtap to create and edit notes:

  • All notes are saved locally using macOS system preferences storage
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No internet connection is required to use the app
  • We cannot access your notes at any time

Third-Party Services

Flashtap does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we don't collect personal information, your data security is maintained by your device's built-in security features.

Children's Privacy

Flashtap does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The app is a general utility tool that does not require any personal information to function.
